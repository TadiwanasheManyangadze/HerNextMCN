What Girls Can Do

What Girls Can Do (WGCD) is an initiative aimed at empowering women, particularly students, to pursue careers in technology by providing them with the necessary skills and platforms for collaboration. The project focuses on increasing awareness of various tech roles and opportunities available to women, fostering an environment of support and mentorship. By encouraging women to enter tech fields, WGCD aims to break stereotypes, promote gender equality in the workplace,and contribute to a more inclusive tech industry.

SheCodesFoundation

SheCodes Foundation is a non-profit organization that aims to provide free coding education to women from developing countries around the world. The foundation offers a 4-month program, SheCodes Plus, which provides access to coding skills at no cost. The goal of the foundation is to bridge the gap in the tech industry by empowering women with the skills needed to succeed in the digital world.The foundation offers a 4-month program, SheCodes Plus, which provides access to coding skills at no cost.
